ScratchX

A smart wearable designed to treat sufferers of OCD

New

ScratchX is a wearable device that uses a multitude of sensors to detect when users are performing destructive actions, like skin picking or hair pulling. The device communicates with the user's smartphone and notifies them of this behavior.

ScratchX has recieved funding from the LEAP foundation and is currently in development

VidPicks - Video Reviews

VidPicks is a real-time video review platform. Hand-selected reviewers post videos from Youtube and Vimeo and share their reviews with the world.
